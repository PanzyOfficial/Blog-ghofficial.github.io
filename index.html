This is Tema Music Festival

Ghana’s most awarded rapper, Sarkodie, has thrilled fans with the announcement of the grand return of the ‘This Is Tema Festival’ for 2023. After a hiatus, the festival is making a comeback, and Sarkodie, a proud Tema boy, took to Instagram to share the news1. The event is scheduled for December 1st to 2nd at Chemu Park in Tema Community 4, promising to be bigger and better than ever.
